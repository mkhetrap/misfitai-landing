Here's how we work:

Scan your wardrobe

Take a quick video or a few photos of your closet when you first log in. We automatically build your digital closet, capturing each item’s color, fabric, fit, and size.

Sync your calendar

Connect your Google Calendar once. misfitAI understands your schedule so it knows what kind of day you're dressing for.

Pick your outfit

Each morning you get a few stylish outfit suggestions tailored to your day. Accept one, swap a piece, or skip — your choice. Every day we learn your style and improve our recommendations.

You keep control over your look. We kill the decision fatigue.
